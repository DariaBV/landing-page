I'm Chloe Vine, a photographer from Montreal. I deal with food, interiors and lifestyle. Now I live in New York. I have 10 years of experience as a professional photographer for various publications and campaigns in North America and Europe.

I graduated as a photographer in Montreal, where I quickly made a name for myself working in the field. Continuing my studies, I transferred to one of the most prestigious schools in the world, Parsons School of Design in New York, where I received a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in photography. I work with many companies in New York. My commercial work ranges from lifestyle to food photography, but maintains a consistent flow and aesthetic.
